姓名：Daniel（已匹配）

ASC编号：

年龄：26

国籍：西班牙

目前教育水平：大学毕业

语言：西班牙语，英语，法语，俄语

兴趣爱好：有礼貌，有活力，开朗的大男孩Daniel，是一个博学，兴趣广泛的典型西班牙人，会吹小号，打鼓（曾在一个乐队里担任鼓手8年），喜欢锻炼，经常去健身房，经常晨跑，喜欢骑车，武术，喜欢古典音乐和爵士乐，还喜欢阅读与历史和工艺相关的书籍。

ASC推荐： 另外，喜欢孩子的他经常在空闲时间帮助朋友照看孩子，也经常和自己的堂弟堂妹们玩以及照顾他们。Daniel还喜欢小动物，自己也养了一只金丝雀和一对情侣鹦鹉。重视家庭的他，经常在父亲的农场帮忙。小时候开始就帮家里做一点力所能及的家务，因为Daniel说，小朋友帮家里做家务在西班牙是很正常并且很普遍的，也是应该的。喜欢中国文化的他，非常希望能够来到中国，感受另一种文化，他说这将是天赐的礼物，如果可以来中国做互惠生，感受中国文化。

自我推荐： Dear family, My name is Daniel and I would like to describe myself with this letter. It was always a great dream of mine to live and work in a foreign country and I am really excited that it will become reality soon. I would like to go to China because it is a beautiful country with its beautiful lakes, mountains and spectacular buildings. I am fascinated by the marvelous landscape and from the nice people living in China. I participated in an “exchange” where a group of young Austrians came to Spain during one month and then we went to Austria. There, we made activities: to play with children, to teach some words in English while we played and spend the day together. It is not always easy to take care of children but it is a great experience to share some time with them and I have much patience. By other way, I’m polite, cheerful and funny and it will be a dream for me to go to China and to know really how families live there. I hope that I will be the right person to take care of your children and see you soon!
